Relative Directions

Each of the relative directions is based on the direction that the Jeroo is facing.
The directions are used as arguments to various methods that need to consider the Jeroo's current location, or an adjacent cell.

LEFT
Ninety degrees to the left of the Jeroo's current direction.
RIGHT
Ninety degrees to the right of the Jeroo's current direction.
AHEAD
This direction is used to refer to the space immediately ahead of the Jeroo.
HERE
This direction is used to refer to the Jeroo's current location.


Compass Directions

Each of the compass directions is an absolute direction. The names are self-explanatory.

WEST EAST
